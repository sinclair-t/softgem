Halo Financial Services Limited Migration

Halo Financial Services Limited offers a wide range of digital financial products, including banking, savings, investments, and credit services. The platform empowers individuals, businesses, and community groups to manage finances, grow savings, and make investments, while providing tailored financial solutions.

Industry Vertical


Digital Financial Services

The Challenges


  • As Halo’s customer base rapidly expanded, they faced two major challenges when transitioning to AWS:

  • Scalability: Halo needed a more flexible infrastructure to support its growing user base and increased demand for services.

  • Cost Optimization: Halo sought to reduce operational costs by migrating to AWS and leveraging its cost-effective solutions to enhance budget management and operational efficiency.

Solution


  • Amazon EC2: Scalable cloud computing power that allowed Halo to manage increasing demand, providing flexible resources for different workloads.

  • Amazon S3: Secure and highly available storage solution for large amounts of data, including user information, transaction records, and financial data, ensuring durability and accessibility.

  • Amazon Managed Blockchain:A fully managed blockchain service to ensure secure, transparent, and decentralized management of financial transactions and smart contracts, enhancing the integrity of Halo’s platform.

Results

  • Increased Scalability:With Amazon EC2, Halo was able to scale its infrastructure dynamically, handling a 40% increase in user activity without compromising performance.

  • Cost Savings: By leveraging AWS’s cost-effective services, Halo reduced its operational costs by 25%, helping optimize their financial operations and budget management.

  • Enhanced Security and Transparency: Using Amazon Managed Blockchain, Halo improved transaction security and transparency, providing their users with more trust in the platform’s integrity. This contributed to a 15% increase in customer satisfaction related to transaction trust
