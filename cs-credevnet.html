Industry Vertical


Software Services and Digital Transformation

The Challenges


Credevnet wanted to create a fast, reliable, and scalable e-commerce platform using AWS. The key challenges were:


Handling Large Amounts of Data: The platform needed to manage high traffic and real-time transactions smoothly.


Ensuring Continuous Access and Secure Data: It was important to keep the platform running without interruptions and protect customer data.


Streamlining Development: Credevnet needed to make the development and deployment process faster and more efficient, reducing downtime.


Solution


  • Amazon DynamoDB: A fast, reliable database for handling large amounts of product and customer data.

  • Amazon S3: A secure storage system for product catalogs, orders, and customer information, ensuring data is always available and protected.

  • DevOps Setup: A simplified development process for quicker updates, better management, and less downtime.

Results

  • Scalability and Performance:The platform handled a 300% increase in traffic seamlessly, ensuring that transaction times stayed low and performance remained stable, even during peak hours.

  • High Availability and Resilience: With [99.99% uptime] ensured by Amazon S3 and DynamoDB, the platform provided reliable access to product catalogs and customer orders without interruptions.

  • Efficient Deployment and Reduced Downtime: The new DevOps setup reduced deployment times by 40%, and downtime during updates was reduced by 60%, allowing the client to push new features and updates without major service interruptions.
