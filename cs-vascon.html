Vascon Solutions ERP

Vascon Solutions is a Nigerian ICT company specializing in Value-Added Solutions Delivery across Enterprise Technology and Renewable Energy. The company offers a broad spectrum of IT solutions such as ERP, CRM, collaboration, telecom mediation services, and renewable energy solutions through its Vascon Energy division. Partnering with global leaders like Microsoft, Cisco, IBM, and Oracle, Vascon Solutions provides innovative, adaptable solutions tailored to meet diverse business needs.

Industry Vertical


Information and Communications Technology (ICT) & Renewable Energy

The Challenges


As Vascon Solutions grew, so did its client base and operational demands. The company needed to migrate its critical ERP applications to AWS to achieve the following goals:


  • Enhance Scalability: The infrastructure needed to support growing business demands, ensuring that resources could scale up or down as needed.

  • Optimize Costs: To reduce operational expenses by using AWS’s cost-efficient solutions, ensuring resources were allocated effectively.

  • Achieve High Resilience: To improve the reliability of their business-critical applications and minimize service downtime.

Solution


  • Amazon EC2:Flexible and scalable compute resources to run the ERP system, supporting seamless expansion and high performance..

  • Amazon CloudWatch: Real-time monitoring and alerts to ensure application performance was optimized and service downtime was minimized.

  • Amazon RDS for MySQL and Oracle: Managed relational databases that provided secure, scalable, and high-performance database solutions to handle the ERP’s critical data.

Results

  • Scalability: The AWS infrastructure enabled Vascon to scale seamlessly, accommodating a 30% increase in business operations and client interactions without performance loss..

  • Cost Optimization: By leveraging AWS's pay-as-you-go model and optimized resource usage, Vascon Solutions achieved a 20% reduction in infrastructure costs.

  • High Resilience: With Amazon EC2 and RDS, Vascon experienced a 99.9% uptime rate, reducing application downtime by 40% and enhancing service reliability for its clients.
